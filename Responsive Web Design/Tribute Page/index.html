Saluting India's Heroes: Remembering the Fallen of 26/11

Their sacrifice will forever inspire us. Their courage will never be forgotten.

Some of the Heroic Acts of Our India's Children

  • Two attackers opened fire at the CSMT around 21:30, killing 58 people and injuring 104 others, but a railway announcer Vishnu Dattaram Zende alerted the passengers to leave the station and saved many lives.
  • Officers Hemant Karkare, Vijay Salaskar, Ashok Kamte were shot dead when they were on their way to the Cama Hospital.
  • Police officer Tukaram Omble was also killed when he tried to disarm Kasab by wrestling his weapon away from him.
  • Sandra Samuel, the Indian nanny saved the life of 2 year old Moshe Holtzberg when the Nariman House was attacked.
  • Major Sandeep Unnikrishnan of the NSG was deployed at the Taj Hotel, succumbed to his injuries after involving in a fierce gun battle.
  • NSG Commando Gajendra Singh was killed in action during the rescue mission at the Taj Hotel.
  • The nobel and loyal manager of the Taj Hotel, Karambir Singh Kang did not abandon his responsibilities even after losing his family(who were trapped in a room engulfed by fire and later died of suffocation), and helped his guests and staff out of the hotel.
  • Mohammed Taufeeq Sheikh, a young boy who ran a tea stall outside CSMT, was among the first to help transport the injured to St. George Hospital.
